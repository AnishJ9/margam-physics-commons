# Company Formation Template

A conditioning layer is an asset. This template helps you map the team needed to get it from a trained model to a paying customer.

Fill this out together as a founding team — or fill out what you know and post it as an open call for the roles you still need.

---

## The Domain

**What physical system are you building around?**

**What is the ground truth signal?**
The thing that tells you whether the physics was right.

**Who is the customer?**
The person or organization who pays for the deviation score.

**What decision does the deviation score inform?**
Be specific. Not "safety" — which decision, made by whom, at what point in the workflow.

## The Commercial Path

**Who signs the first check?**
Name the role (CMO, VP Operations, Chief Safety Officer) and the organization type.

**What is the procurement path?**
How does a company like your customer actually buy something like this?

**What is the price point?**
Annual license, per-use, per-seat, or outcome-based?
