# Role: Sales / Business Development

---

## What You Bring

You know how a customer in your domain actually buys something. You know who signs the check, who blocks the deal, what objections come up, and what a pilot looks like. You don't need to understand the ODE. You need to understand the buying process.

---

## What You Contribute

- Identify the right buyer (role, organization type, budget holder)
- Map the procurement path — how does a company like your target actually acquire a product like this?
- Run outreach and own the first conversation
- Structure the pilot offer — what does the customer commit to, what do they get, what does success look like?
- Close the first deal and document what worked

---

## What You Receive From the Team

- **From Domain Expert:** the ground truth claim — "our deviation score predicts X before it happens" — in language a buyer understands
- **From Product:** the workflow description — where the deviation score fits in the customer's existing process
- **From Regulatory:** what the product is and is not (classification, approved use) so you don't over-promise
- **From Marketing:** the one-pager, the pitch deck, the demo

---

## What You Produce

- First paid pilot agreement
- Customer discovery notes — what the buyer actually cares about, what language they use, what objections they raise
- Procurement map for your domain

---

## How This Connects to the Marketplace

Sales is the last mile. A conditioning layer in the marketplace earns nothing until someone buys it. The sales role closes the loop between "physics was right" and "customer paid."

Revenue from a licensed layer gets distributed to the full team, including the sales role. The split is negotiated in `build/COMPANY_TEMPLATE.md` before the first deal closes.

---

## What AI Can Do Here

AI can draft outreach, prep discovery questions, simulate objections, and research procurement patterns at target organizations. You still have to be in the room. AI handles the preparation.

---

## To Get Started

1. Fill out the Sales section of `build/COMPANY_TEMPLATE.md` for your domain
2. Read the domain expert's `contribute/DOMAIN_TEMPLATE.md` so you understand what you're selling
3. Post your open role in your team's `build/companies/` file if you need a team, or find one that needs you
