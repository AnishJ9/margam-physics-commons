# Role: Operations

---

## What You Bring

You make the company actually work. Coordination between roles, customer onboarding, pilot management, payment tracking, issue triage. The physics layer can be perfect and the company can still fail because nobody is keeping track of what needs to happen next.

---

## What You Contribute

- Own the coordination between roles — who needs what from whom, by when
- Manage the pilot: customer onboarding, check-ins, success criteria tracking
- Track marketplace revenue and confirm payment distribution is correct
- Triage issues from customers and route them to the right role
- Keep `build/companies/your_domain/` up to date as the company evolves

---

## What You Receive From the Team

- **From Product:** the pilot design and success criteria
- **From Sales:** the signed pilot agreement and customer contact
- **From Finance:** the payment tracking setup

---

## What You Produce

- A pilot tracker: customer, start date, success criteria, status
- A roles matrix: who owns what, who covers what when someone is unavailable
- Regular status updates to the team

---

## What AI Can Do Here

Claude Code can draft trackers, status update templates, and coordination documents. The operations judgment — what's actually blocked, what needs escalation, what a customer is really saying when they go quiet — is yours.

---

## To Get Started

1. Read `build/COMPANY_TEMPLATE.md` — fill in or verify all roles are covered
2. Set up the coordination structure: how does the team communicate, how often, what's the escalation path?
3. Own the pilot tracker from day one — don't wait until you're in the middle of a pilot to build it
