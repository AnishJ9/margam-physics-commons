# Roles in the Physics Commons

Every role that helps get a physics layer to a customer belongs here. The list is not fixed. If your function matters to shipping, add it.

---

## How to Add a Role

1. Copy any existing role template in this directory
2. Fill in: what this role contributes, what it receives from other roles, what it produces, how it connects to the eval/marketplace cycle
3. Submit as `build/roles/your-role-name.md`

---

## Current Roles

| Role | File | What They Do |
|------|------|--------------|
| Domain Expert | `domain-expert.md` | Knows what "physics was right" means. Writes the causal structure. |
| Data Engineer | `data-engineer.md` | Finds, accesses, and preprocesses the dataset. |
| ML Engineer | `ml-engineer.md` | Trains and evaluates the conditioning layer. |
| Product | `product.md` | Defines the product around the deviation score. Owns the customer workflow. |
| Sales / BD | `sales.md` | Knows the procurement path. Gets in the room. Closes the first deal. |
| Marketing | `marketing.md` | Positions the deviation score. Translates physics into language buyers understand. |
| Regulatory | `regulatory.md` | Maps the approval or exemption path. Owns the evidence package. |
| Finance | `finance.md` | Models unit economics. Structures the company. Manages revenue attribution. |
| Legal | `legal.md` | Handles pilot agreements, licensing, IP assignment. |
| Operations | `operations.md` | Runs the day-to-day. Makes sure the company actually works. |

---

## Open Roles Across Teams

Teams posting open roles do so in their `build/companies/` file. Check there if you're looking to join a team.

If you have a role that doesn't fit any of the above, define it here first, then find or form a team that needs it.
